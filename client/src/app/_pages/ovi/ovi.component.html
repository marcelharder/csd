Online Valve Inventory

This application gives a real time overview of the available prosthetic heart valves in your hospital. It allows a cardiac surgeon to plan the operative procedure with the confidence that the intended prosthesis is really available. On the other hand it gives valve vendors a realtime overview of the location of their products. This enables them to intelligently supply the hospitals that they are working with.
